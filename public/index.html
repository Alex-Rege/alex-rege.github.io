About Me

I am an applied mathematician working on models in kinetic theory. I am particularly interested in models for magnetized plasmas, such as the magnetized Vlasov-Poisson system and the Vlasov-Maxwell system. From 2021 to 2024, I was a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics of ETH Zürich under the supervision of Mikaela Iacobelli. Before this I was a PhD student working at the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions in Paris under the supervision of Bruno Després and Frédérique Charles. Here is my PhD manuscript and the slides of my defense.
